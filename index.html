Our Mission

"To create medical facilities to provide primary medical care in remote rural area, to minimize urban expensive and prohibitive medical treatment by providing it at subsidized rates or free. No patient will be denied medical treatment due to his financial incapability of paying for it. To maintain high standard of medical ethics while providing adequate, timely and scientific medical treatment, and to educate patients by providing pertinent information about their illness and available treatment"
